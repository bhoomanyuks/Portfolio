About Me

Design is how I
think out loud

I'm Bhoomanyu Sajeevkumar, a graphic designer and video editor from Thrissur, Kerala. I completed my Professional Diploma in Multimedia Animation at Smart Media College, training in the full Adobe Creative Suite alongside Print & Layout Design tools.

My work spans poster design, brochures, coupons, social media creatives, and video editing. I'm drawn to clean, purposeful design — work that communicates clearly without unnecessary noise.

Alongside design, I hold a BCom Finance degree — which means I understand business context, not just pixels. I know what a brand is trying to say, and I know how to say it visually.
